About Event

Minders’20 Mentorships event is to contribute to achieve Minders’ mission, by producing young entrepreneurs, through developing an adequate and strong foundation in different fields by providing the assistance of professional mentors.

Target

This event targets anyone from any faculty, especially students who are looking for career advice to help them with a roadmap provided by professional mentors.
